Our Services

Custom Guitar Building

Design Consultation: Discussing your vision, including body shape, neck profile, wood types, and aesthetics. Custom Build: Crafting a guitar from scratch based on your specifications, including selecting tonewoods, building the neck, and finishing the body.

Repairs and Restoration

Setup and Adjustment: Tweaking the guitar’s action, intonation, and truss rod to improve playability. Refretting: Replacing or leveling frets to ensure smooth playability. Neck Repairs: Fixing issues such as warping, cracks, or breaks in the neck. Body Repairs: Addressing cracks, dents, and other damage to the guitar’s body.

Maintenance

Cleaning and Conditioning: Removing grime and applying conditioners to the fretboard and body. Electronics Check: Inspecting and repairing wiring, pickups, and other electronic components.

Upgrades

Pickups: Installing or replacing pickups to alter the guitar’s sound. Hardware: Upgrading parts like bridges, tuners, and knobs for better performance or aesthetics. Finish Work: Refinishing the guitar’s body to change its appearance or restore its original look.

Consulting and Assessment

Evaluation: Assessing a guitar’s condition and recommending necessary repairs or upgrades. Value Appraisal: Determining the value of a guitar, often for insurance purposes or resale.
